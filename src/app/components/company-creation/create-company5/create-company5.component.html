List of documents required for your applications

1. Documents for DIN (Director Identification Number):

  1. Copy of PAN for resident of India (mandatory)
  2. Copy of passport size photograph (four copies)
  3. Proof of residence (like voter card, passport, driving license, electricity bill, telephone bill or bank account statement). Proof of residence should not be older than 2 months/ 60 days.
  4. Digital signature for the director
In case individual is a foreign national, copy of passport will be mandatory along with other proof. For foreign nationals all documents need to be notarized and apostilled in their respective home country.

2. Documents for registered office of the company:

  1. Copy of lease deed
  2. Copy of rent paid not older than one month
  3. Utility bill (like telephone bill, water bill, electricity bill)
  4. NOC letter in case of rented premises to be obtained from the landlord along with proof of ownership or occupancy authorization
  5. In case of owned premises copy of sale deed

Please note for DSC, the below documents will be required separately

A. For Indian residents:

  1. Passport Size Photos to be affixed on the DSC applicatiton.
  2. Copy of PAN (to be attested by Banker/Gazetted Officer)
  3. Copy of Passport/Adhaar Card/Bank Statement/Driving license (current address proof). To be attested by Banker/Gazetted Officer mentioning the phone number
  4. DSC application duly filled and signed

B. For foreign nationals:

  1. Passport Size Photos to be affixed on the DSC applicatiton.
  2. Copy of Passport (current address proof). Mandatory.
  3. Any other poof of identity
  4. DSC application duly filled and signed
All documents mentioned in point 1 to 4 (including photo on DSC) needs to be Consularized by Native Country, after Public Notary.
